Messenger 3.0

New Messenger 3.0

Author: Alexandra Gavrilova
Last updated: November 5, 2025

The messenger has become the central section for collaboration. Upon entering Bitrix24, the user lands in chats — this helps to immediately engage in communication with colleagues and quickly get involved in current tasks. A newcomer does not need to navigate the interface to start working.
Messenger: interface and features

In this article, we will discuss the main features of the messenger:

  • multi-space for collaboration,
  • secure chats,
  • polls in chats,
  • quick access to information,
  • BitrixGPT — a work assistant.


Multi-space for collaboration

Messenger 3.0 is a unified workspace where tools are interconnected. There is no need to install and configure different applications — Bitrix24 has everything you need for work: chats, tasks, calendar, documents, and other tools.

You can create tasks and meetings in chats from specific messages or without linking to them. You can switch from the chat to the task card and vice versa. Switching between tools happens without unnecessary clicks and windows. This helps the team save time and work productively on any devices.
How to create a task or meeting from a message

In the chat, you can forward messages, pin them, and add them to favorites. Stay close to your colleagues even remotely: share emotions in chats, react to messages.


Secure chats

In chats, only company employees are present.New employees are automatically added to the chats and channels of their department, while dismissed employees lose access to Bitrix24.

An outsider cannot write or call in the chat, impersonate a colleague, and gain access to the correspondence. This protects against phishing attacks, spam, and data leaks.
Chats in Bitrix24: how to choose the right one

Automatic message deletion. Set the retention period for correspondence after which messages in the chat will disappear by themselves. This is important if you discuss confidential topics, personal data, or temporary tasks that quickly lose relevance.
How to set up auto-deletion of messages in Bitrix24 chats
How to set up auto-deletion of messages in the mobile app


Polls in chats

Polls in Bitrix24 help quickly gather employee opinions, collect feedback, and make decisions based on real data. For example, to choose a date for a corporate event, gather feedback on a new process, or decide which logo is better.
Polls in Bitrix24 chats: how to create and configure

Polls can be created:

  • in group chats,
  • channels,
  • collabs.


Quick access to information

Messages, files, and video call recordings remain in the chat and can be found at any time through the sidebar. The sidebar is the side panel of the chat. Here you can access:

  • favorite messages,
  • media and files,
  • links from messages,
  • tasks,
  • meetings.

From favorite messages and links in the sidebar, you can navigate to the context of the discussion. To see the message from which the task was created, click on it.
How to open the sidebar

Voice messages can be rewound and playback speed can be changed to quickly return to the desired moment.


BitrixGPT — a work assistant

BitrixGPT is integrated into daily work and can act as an expert in a specific area. We have added more than 30 roles: sales department manager, lawyer, HR, and others. You can use these roles or create your own.

BitrixGPT will help solve various tasks:

  • formulate a goal and break it down into stages,
  • prepare the structure of a presentation or report,
  • highlight the main points from a large text,
  • understand a complex situation.

  • The messenger has become the central section for collaboration. Upon entering Bitrix24, the user lands in chats — this helps to immediately engage in communication with colleagues and quickly get involved in current tasks.
  • Messenger 3.0 is a unified workspace where tools are interconnected. There is no need to install and configure different applications — Bitrix24 has everything you need for work: chats, tasks, calendar, documents, and other tools.
  • New employees are automatically added to their department's chats, while dismissed employees lose access to Bitrix24.
  • The chat now has the option for automatic message deletion. Set the retention period for correspondence after which messages will disappear by themselves.
  • Polls in chats help quickly gather employee opinions and make decisions.
  • Messages, files, and video call recordings remain in the chat and can be found at any time through the sidebar.
